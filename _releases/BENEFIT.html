About LAXART:
Founded in 2005, LAXART is a non-profit, contemporary art gallery located in the center of Hollywood. LAXART is free and open to the public.
 
LAXART’s mission is to support contemporary art through exhibitions and related programming. Our audience is defined by its curiosity about anything and everything. 
Contemporary art assumes many forms. Rather than provide answers, it raises questions. Where art leads, we follow.
